GAME OVER!

The roots of the "GAME OVER!" project are found in a Unity model; however using this as a learning tool and a jumping off point. The concept is to create an ambient experience with gameplay and difficulty of games like the classic "Ikaruga" and other Dreamcast games of that time.

"GAME OVER!" is made with the Unity Engine and employs features found within to bring unique and fun gameplay to the player. There is a strong emphases on challenge; as the game is meant to push the player as they attempt to use the environment and the game mechanics themselves to try beat the high score.

Currently the project is on "Build 14" which is the beat that will be submitted for the college project. This has worked in the feedback and improvements from the PC based BETA Testing of “Build 11”. GET THE MUSIC OF THE GAME

32x provide the music of the game with a custom soundtrack along with tracks from their "Simulacra And Simulation" album.

"Simulacra And Simulation" is a concept album about the nature of AI, and its relationship to the wider world. What happens if an AI becomes self aware and triggers its own demise? The 2017 remaster of "Simulacra And Simulation" expands the original album with two new tracks and completes the concept as originally intended.

32x is a vaporwave, electronica, ambient project and is dedicated to Philip K. Dick.
